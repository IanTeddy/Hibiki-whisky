響 (HIBIKI) JAPAN HARMONY

Suntory Whisky "HIBIKI Japanese Harmony" resonates with the harmony of Japan's nature, the delicate sensibilities of the Japanese people, and the craftsmanship of Japan's artisans. It embodies the unique value created by the harmonization of Japan's spiritual culture throughout the changing seasons. "Hibiki Japanese Harmony" resonates with the world as the crystallization of Japanese beauty.

Tasting Note

A soft taste that spreads gorgeously. Beautifully balanced harmony unique to Japanese whiskey.

Color Amber
Aroma Rose, lychee, hint of rosemary, aged oak, sandalwood
Taste Clear sweetness of honey, orange peel chocolate
Aftertaste Delicate, gently and gently lingering, subtle quercus

響 (HIBIKI) Blender's Choice

We have carefully selected a wide range of sake aged in various barrels to deepen the maturation, and layered delicate blends with the skills of a craftsman. The result is a rich and sweet flavor with a deep complexity, accompanied by a vibrant and soft fragrance. Enjoy the exquisite harmony born from the addition of wine barrel-aged sake.

Sweetness derived from the wine barrel aged original. Rich woodiness and a deep, mellow, mature aroma. The lingering finish with a hint of bitterness lingers gently.

Color Reddish amber

Taste Full-bodied sweetness, pleasant acidity

響 (HIBIKI) 21 Years Old

The malt sake used in the blend is carefully selected and blended from super long-aged malt sake that is 21 years old or older. The grain base sake used was also carefully selected and blended with matured base sake that is 21 years old or older. You can enjoy the luxurious harmony of the original sake, made possible by the wide variety of Suntory's original sake.

Tasting Note

The aroma is extremely fruity and luscious, with an aged flavor reminiscent of flowers. The mouthfeel is smooth, with the 21-year old wine's heavy body and graceful richness. It has a long, deep and lingering aftertaste.

Color Slightly dark amber
Aroma Raisins, apricots, strawberry jam, vanilla
Taste Biscuity, toffee, sweet and sour, spicy
Aftertaste Ripe fruit aroma, pleasant and long finish

響 (HIBIKI) 30 Years Old

Only a few thousand bottles are made per year, making it a rare and precious limited edition. For the malt base sake, malt base sake that is at least 30 years old is carefully selected from Suntory's treasured ultra-long maturation casks. This is then carefully blended with matured grain base sake that is at least 30 years old. Because there are only a small number of bottles available, the entire process is done by hand. It is the pinnacle of Suntory blended whiskey and is highly regarded by whiskey fans around the world as a luxurious jewel of beauty.

Tasting Note

Sweet aroma derived from ultra-long aged malt, rich in floral aroma reminiscent of flowers. Smooth on the palate. Heavy and full-bodied. Powerful woodiness. Long-lasting aromatic aftertaste.

Color Dark amber
Aroma Mango, apricot, strawberry jam
Taste Thickness, thickness, sweet and sour
Aftertaste Ripe fruit aroma, Kyara aroma, long and deep finish
